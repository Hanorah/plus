A little about Church plus

"Founded in 2005 by Reverend Emmanuel Ubamadu, our community at Church Plus is built upon a foundation of faith, unity, and service. Dedicated to fostering spiritual growth, nurturing families, and extending compassion to all, we strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone can find belonging, guidance, and inspiration in their faith journey."
"At Church Plus, family is the heart of our values, fostering love, unity, and growth in faith. As a proud branch of the Church of God Mission, our spiritual foundation is strengthened by our affiliation. Under the guidance of Reverend Carl Umakhihe, our current leader, we continue to uphold the mission of nurturing strong family bonds, offering support, and promoting spiritual development within our congregation and the wider community."
